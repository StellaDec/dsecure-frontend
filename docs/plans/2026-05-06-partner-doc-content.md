# Design Doc: D-Secure Partner Presentation Content

**Date**: 2026-05-06  
**Topic**: Partner Slide Deck (3-4 Slides)  
**Tone**: Practical, No-nonsense, Honest  

## Objective
Provide high-quality, practical slide content for a partner/client presentation that explains D-Secure's value proposition without using fake claims or complex designs.

## Content Strategy
1. **Slide 1: What We Do (Simple)**
   - Clear focus on File/Drive Erasure and Compliance.
   - Use direct terminology (Industrial Erasure Engine).
2. **Slide 2: Why Useful for ITAD**
   - Emphasize efficiency (Parallel processing).
   - Legal/Audit safety (Certified reports).
3. **Slide 3: How Partner Works**
   - Outline the partner-client-software relationship.
   - Mention revenue flexibility to keep options open.
4. **Slide 4: Current Traction (Honest)**
   - State "Early Stage" but "Product Ready".
   - Specific mention of India/UAE discussions to provide regional context.

## Constraints
- **English only** as requested.
- **No changes to website pages** (content only).
- **Practical & Non-fancy**.

## Deliverable
- A Markdown artifact containing the slide-by-slide copy for the user to copy-paste into their presentation tool.
